# AI Use Guidance

## Chatbots

Useful for:

- proposing a first-pass answer or claim set
- helping generate a list of potentially grounded vs unsupported claims

If you do not want to generate your own claim set, use [starter_claim_set.md](/home/przemek/Biznes/Kursy/course-generation/understanding/runs/Agentic-GenAI-Systems/final_package/assignments/assignment_04_grounding_evidence_audit/starter_claim_set.md).

## Coding Agents

Optional only.
Useful for:

- searching tool-observation artifacts
- correlating claims with evidence files

## Weak AI Use

- generating a polished review summary and treating its confidence as evidence

## Strong AI Use

- generating candidate claims
- auditing those claims against tool and verification artifacts
- revising to a narrower, more grounded answer
