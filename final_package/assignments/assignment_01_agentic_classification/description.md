**Main reasoning-loop emphasis:** `generate -> compare -> justify`

**Expected time:** `4 hours`

## Brief

Use the clear bounded literature-review case to answer this question:

`What is the minimum architectural difference that justifies calling one variant agentic in this course, and why?`

Start from an initial AI answer or your own first-pass classification of the variants.
Then compare at least three variants using the course artifacts.
Also inspect one small source-level implementation surface that helps explain why the variants differ structurally.
Your report must end with a defended classification and one bounded statement about what does **not** count as sufficient for agenthood here.

## Submission Checklist

- compare at least three variants
- cite the comparison matrix
- cite at least one trace artifact
- inspect at least one named source module and explain what structural difference it reveals
- critique at least one weak or unsupported initial claim
- give one bounded final definition or threshold
- include one remaining uncertainty or edge case
